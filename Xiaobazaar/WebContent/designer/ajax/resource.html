Designing a theme is easy! Here's how!

1) Download the Theme creation kit here

2) Save the file to your desired location:

3) Unzip the file to your desired working directory:


4) Once opened, make all the desired changes in the file "theme.css":


5) Right click theme.css and open with an HTML editor (eg notepad++):


6) Design to your heart's content!


7) Save the file once you have done editing it


8) Open index.html to verify your changes


9) Go to the Theme Manager tab in your home screen. Start by giving your Theme a name, selecting a category and stating a price.

10) Next, click on the Upload Theme button and find the directory where your edited 'theme.css' file was saved.

11) Remember to press the submit button once your theme is successfully uploaded and you're done!

12) Our friendly administrators will review your submitted theme within 1-3 working days. If accepted, your theme will be ready for purchase on the theme marketplace.
